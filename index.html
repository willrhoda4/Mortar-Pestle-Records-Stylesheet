Text Styles

H1: Main Page Heading

Florent Bold Type shall be used for most main headings, although Florent Poster Two Outline will be considered an acceptable, ocassional alternative for black-background applications.

  • Font-weight: normal
  • Font-size: xx-large
  • Font-family: Florent-Poster-Two

H2: Subheading

Open Sans Extra-Bold shall be the font of choice for subheadings and similar applications.

  • Font-weight: 500
  • Font-size: x-large
  • Font-family: Open-Sans-Extra-Bold

Paragraph Text

Open Sans shall be the font of choice for body copy and other miscellaneous small text.

  • Font-weight: normal
  • Font-size: large
  • Font-family: Open Sans
